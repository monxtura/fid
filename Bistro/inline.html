Low and Slow

This week I am extremly excited about a new cooking technique called sous vide. In sous vide cooking, you submerge the food (usually vacuum-sealed in plastic) into a water bath that is precisely set to target tempreature you want the food to be cooked to. In his book, Cooking for Geek, Jeff Potter describes it as ultra-low-tempreature poaching.
